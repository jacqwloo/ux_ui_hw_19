ABOUT ME

meet me, wenli!

A passionate and colourful Naarm based aspiring UX/UI Designer, and wannabe Graphic Designer with degrees in Information Technology (Interactive Media) and Arts (Film & TV Studies).

I enjoy creating fun, accessible, and unique designs, inspired by my love of film, television and music.

When problem solving, I love to empathise and figure out the most effective way of solving for x, employing my critical thinking and communication skills to deep dive into the thoughts and needs of my user.

In my free time, I love perusing the city for the best sandwiches, and watching the latest cinematic releases.
